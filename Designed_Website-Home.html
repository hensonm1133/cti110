Our Philosophy is All Parents Can Teach Their Children

Children's curriculum is constantly changing and evolving. New studies on how children learn pushes the evolution of learning in instituational schools. For example, the adoption of the Common Core Standards has changed older methods of mathematics. This causes parents grow frustrated thinking of ways to help their child. Other times a child doesn't understand a parent's method. This comes from different learning and teaching styles. We believe that you can teach your child read without using expensive programs. We aim to guide you and reduce frustrations through the resources we have available for you on our website. Teaching or tutoring isn't as hard as you think. Homeworkcan be made easy.

At No Fuss Homework, we help you explore three different learning styles to help you find how your child learns. Children, and people in general, retain content being taught best through visual, auditory, or kinesthetic methods. Retention is even greater when using multiple methods.

Different Learning Styles


Visual Learners

Learn best when taught with illustrations, graphics, video demonstrations, and flash card methods.


Auditory Learners

Learn best when taught through auditory means. This could mean using audio books, recorded lectures, or recording text to be played for later use.


Kinesthetic Learners

Learn best with manipulatives, hands-on activities, and moving while studying.


Multiple Methods

Using multiple methods help children retain the study material.
