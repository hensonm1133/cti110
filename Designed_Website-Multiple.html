Using Multiple Methods


List of helpful tool

Reading while listening to audiobook or music

This ties in two methods of learning helping a child remember what is read. Listening to music can also help a child focus.


Using graphic cards along with manipulatives

This helps children see and grasp the concept.


Songs and flash cards

Joining songs with flash cards/graphic posters can help trigger visuals when trying to recall.
