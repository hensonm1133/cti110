Kinesthetic Learners


List of helpful tool

Manipulatives

Manipulatives are great aid in helping to count, multiple, and divide.


Hands-on Activities

Touching and grasping items help children experience more than just hearing or reading about a subject alone.


Movement

Some children need movement to focus. Moving around, sitting on a swiss ball, or playing with a fidget toy can help increase focus.
