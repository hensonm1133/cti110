Auditory Learners


List of helpful tool

Audio Books

Audiobooks can help auditory learners retain what they read.


Record Lectures

Ask your child's teacher if she would be willing to record her lectures.


Recording Text

When studying for literature, record yourself or your child reading their literature book. This allows children who have difficulty with reading to hear the story while simultaneously reading.
